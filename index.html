NETWORK OF WOMEN


N.O.W. stands for Network of Women. This is an initiative by ThoughtWorks, and is a growing and connected community of women. ThoughtWorks N.O.W. is a forum for curious women from all walks of life, looking to be part of an open and inclusive network, to come together to learn from each other and be inspired. It provides a positive and motivating environment by bringing fresh content, wonderful speakers and trainers, and innovative formats.


N.O.W. brings together women from the entrepreneurial to the curious, ambitious and successful leaders. They have a passionate enthusiasm for sharing their choices, their journeys, successes and failures with the larger community of women. And this is the reason for N.O.W. to exist. Come, hear the experiences that have shaped the person, these strong women have become. Learn practical tips and ideas for putting your best and most confident foot forward. Do encourage other women in your network to come and join in the activities and network with us at N.O.W!

Speakers

Balvinder is working as Lead Consultant with ThoughtWorks and has been working in the IT industry for 13 years now. She is a passionate technologist, a doting mother, a homemaker and a woman. She likes to work on challenging projects, where she can make a difference using software, and motivate other technologists by sharing her knowledge. At the same time, she works equally hard to create a positive impact in the lives of people in her personal life.

Sonu Bhugra heads Africa Delivery Operations in LTI and also is founder and Regional Leader for Lean in : Lean2Lead Pune , a women leaders network. She brings around 17+years of Industry experience and passionately drives profitability and revenue maximisation by building operational effectiveness and process automation. Her life mantra is “Focus on you than me”

Niharica is a Psychologist and Facilitator of Art and Movement Therapies. Her passion lies in creating safe spaces for individuals and groups to explore, express and transform themselves and their relationships. She is a firm believer of self-compassion and self-care is an irreplaceable component of emotional first-aid.

Dipsi is working as a Program Manager in Global TechOps team at ThoughtWorks. She started her career in the field of Organisational Development about 10 years ago and eventually shifted to roles that were more business focused. She loves her career and find her bliss in being at work that gives her a sense of belongingness and independence.

Deepali have been with ThoughtWorks now for 11 years and responsible for the Germany market from India. She looks at the demand and delivery side for all work coming from Germany to India. She specializes in distributed delivery and has played various roles like that of project manager, program manager, delivery manager. She also enjoys painting and trying her hand at acrylic painting and rangoli.

Gunjan is a senior management professional with proven expertise in building business and delivering technology programs. She is currently playing the role of Market tech partner for the SEA market from ThoughtWorks India. As part of this she is responsible for managing and growing the portfolio coming in from the SEA region, Prior to this role she was part of the global operations team and worked directly with the Chief Commercial Officers office. Her main responsibilities included scaling demand operations up, across all the countries in which ThoughtWorks has a presence. Gunjan's growth at ThoughtWorks is linked to the several caps that she has donned; Business Analyst, Project Manager, Client Principal, People Champion for the Pune office, General Manager for the Pune office, Demand Operations Lead for India, Chief of Staff for the MD’s office and more recently leading the Engineering for Research practice in India. The cumulative experience spanning 18 years has seen Gunjan gain experience across business functions; Operations, Business Analysis, Client Management, Global Delivery, Offshore Delivery, Delivery Excellence, Operations Leadership Development and Agile Program Management and stand to benefit her in her current role.

Zaineb Ali leads the Corporate Social Responsibility mandate of Eaton group of companies in India. She is also full time engaged as a mother of a toddler. In the past, she has worked with Centre for Migration and Labour Solutions (CMLS) at Aajeevika Bureau; Centre for Insurance and Risk Management at Institute of Financial Management and Research; Ujjivan Microfinance Pvt Ltd and Central Academy School. A trainer, teacher, researcher and practitioner, she has over 13 years of experience working on concerns and vulnerabilities of migrant workers, gender, food insecurity, financial exclusion and socially relevant technological innovation. She has a Master degree in Social Work from Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS) and a Master of Science (Mathematics) from Mohanlal Sukhadia University. Being a foodie, avid gardener and swimmer, she dons many hats.

Hitanshi is an Architect from Vadodara, Gujarat. She is 26, and is currently practicing as an assistant architect at 'Graphite Space Design Studio' (local Architecture firm). She is also an artist and loves to paint and sketch. She is a believer and her philosophy is "Accept everything,Your surrounding, Yourself.. and then, just Go with the Flow."
